About the Game

Bubble Incremental is a feature-rich clicker and idle game where you start small — creating your first few bubbles by hand — and gradually build a massive bubble empire. Purchase upgrades, unlock milestones, discover unique buffs, and experiment with different strategies to maximize your Bubble Production Per Second (BPS).

Whether you enjoy the satisfaction of manual clicking, the strategy of long-term idle builds, or the excitement of chasing achievements and rare buffs, Bubble Incremental offers something for every kind of player.

Core Features

  • Upgrades & Milestones: Boost production with permanent upgrades and milestone bonuses.
  • Buffs: Temporary power boosts like Golden Bubbles, Shooting Stars, and more.
  • Achievements: Earn perks for meeting in-game goals and challenges.
  • Weather & Events: Dynamic in-game conditions that change your strategy.
  • Marketplace: Trade and acquire unique items to further your progress.
  • * Ascension System: Reset your progress for permanent multipliers and new unlocks.

Getting Started

  • Download the latest Bubble Incremental release from GitHub.
  • Open your Downloads folder and extract the .zip file.
  • Launch Incremental.exe and start clicking — every bubble counts!
  • Experiment with upgrades and strategies to grow your bubble empire.
