Marketplace

Overview

The Marketplace is a special area where you can spend bubbles on more than just upgrades. Here you’ll find features like the Trader and the Brewery.

  • Trader – Offers unique items and deals that can boost your progress.
  • Brewery – Lets you brew potions that provide temporary bubble-boosting effects.
