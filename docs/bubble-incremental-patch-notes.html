Beta 1.4.0

Augut 17, 2025

Game Changes

  • Achievements have been assigned colored borders to distinguish between achievements easier.
  • 5 NEW Trader Items
    • Some Names and Descriptions for existing Trader Items have also been refreshed.
  • 2 NEW Weathers
    • Descriptions for existing Weathers have been refreshed.
  • New Buff: Mutated Bubble
    • Gives a +20% × Amount of a RANDOM Item for 30 seconds (e.g., Your 20 Soaps are contributing 400% more towards your production!)
    • However, there is a 20% chance the Mutated Bubble backfires and HALVES your production instead.
  • New Feature: Clicking Hotspots
    • Clicking Hotspots are a new way to earn more Bubbles through, well, clicks!
    • Periodically, a hotspot appears on the clicking area. Clicking on this hotspot gives you an extra ×1.2 multiplier to your Clicks. They appear at random intervals and each lasts anywhere between 5 to 15 seconds.
  • New Feature: The Library
    • Achievements now appear in the Library.

New Items

  • Trader: Wishing Star | ~1/1.15k (0.08695%)
    • Summons a Shooting Star Bubble
    • Passively earn an additional 15% more Bubbles for 20s
  • Trader: Nuclear Waste | 1/1k (0.1%)
    • Summons a Mutated Bubble
    • Hinders your Bubble production by 15% for 30s
  • Trader: Marshy Goodness | 1/250 (0.4%)
    • Passively earn 30% more Bubbles for 30s
    • Clicks are 50% more powerful for 30s
  • Trader: Bubble Beacon | 1/50 (2.0%)
    • Bubbles spawn three times as fast for 60s
    • Passively earn 10% more Bubbles for 60s
  • Trader: Midas Bubbles | 1/500 (0.2%)
    • Summons a RANDOM Golden Bubble
    • Passively earn an additional 5% more Bubbles for 10s

New Weathers

  • Blissful Bubbly Sunshine
    • ×1.15 Bubbles per Second along with making Bubbles take twice as long to spawn.
  • Marshy Springs
    • ×1.5 Bubbles per Second along with making Bubbles spawn 25% faster.

Balance Changes

  • Marketplace Restock Interval | 30s → 60s
  • Bubble Mania Buff | +25% → +20%
