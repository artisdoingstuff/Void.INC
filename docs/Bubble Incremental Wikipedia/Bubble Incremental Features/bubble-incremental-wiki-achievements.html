Achievements

Overview

Achievements in Bubble Incremental are in-game milestones that reward players for completing certain objectives, ranging from simple early-game tasks to advanced long-term goals. Each achievement you unlock grants rewards such as permanent BPS multipliers, click multipliers, and buff spawnrates being decreased. They are a great way to track your progress and set goals while playing.

Achievements are displayed in-game through the Library.

Types of Achievements

  • Clicking Achievements: Earned by actively clicking bubbles. For example, reaching a total of 10,000 clicks.
  • Production Achievements: Achieved by accumulating a certain number of bubbles Such as, generating 100,000,000 bubbles.
  • Milestone Achievements: Unlock bonuses when reaching key item counts or upgrade milestones.
  • Buff Achievements: Awarded for triggering a certain number of buffs.
  • Special Achievements: Rare or secret achievements that often require unique strategies or exploration of advanced game mechanics.

Perks

Unlocking achievements comes with a variety of perks to enhance your gameplay:

  • Permanent BPS Bonuses: Certain achievements grant a multiplier to your overall bubble production.
  • Clicking Bonuses: Clicking achievements can grant bonus multipliers on click to further boost production.
  • Buff Spawnrate Decrease: Some achievements grant the ability to decrease the time it takes between buff spawns.
