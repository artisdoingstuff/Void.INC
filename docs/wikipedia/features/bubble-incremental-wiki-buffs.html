Buffs

Overview

Buffs are temporary effects that change how bubbles are generated or collected. Each buff has its own multiplier, duration, and rarity. Multiple buffs can be active at once, and all are managed by a unified system that handles spawning, timers, and effects.

Global Bubble Rules

Global bubbles are spawned on a queued system. Only one global bubble appears at a time, with the next spawn occurring randomly between 120 and 180 seconds. Clicking these bubbles activates one of the buffs above.

Stacking Rules

Buff multipliers stack multiplicatively with each other and with other game systems such as milestones, upgrades, and achievements. If multiple buffs are active, their effects are compounded.

Strategy Tips

  • Time Multiplicative or Shooting Bubble Star buffs right before making large purchases to maximize efficiency.
  • Use Frenzy as a stable mid-length multiplier, especially in combination with milestones.
  • Chaos and Mayhem are best during active play, where frequent clicks can take advantage of their effects.
  • Save Mutated bubbles for experimental runs or stacking with rare multipliers.
